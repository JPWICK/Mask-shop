Built on Heritage

Raksha & Co. was founded to preserve the dying art of Sri Lankan mask carving. Each piece is crafted from the sacred Kaduru tree, dried in the sun, and painted with traditional techniques passed down through seven generations.

Now based in Japan, we ensure these ceremonial spirits find a home where their craftsmanship is truly appreciated.
